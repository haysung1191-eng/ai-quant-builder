# AI Quant Builder FAQ

## What is AI Quant Builder?

AI Quant Builder is an AI investment research and strategy validation tool. It helps retail investors turn investment ideas into backtested, risk-checked strategy reports before they trade.

## Is this an auto-trading product?

Not in the first MVP. The first product is research-first. It focuses on idea validation, backtesting, risk analysis, and improvement suggestions.

## Does it provide financial advice?

No. The intended positioning is investment research and strategy validation, not personalized financial advice or guaranteed returns.

## Why is the working prototype private?

The working prototype contains local market-data, trading, and account-related workflow code. The public repository is intentionally limited to product direction and accelerator-review materials.

## Who is the first target user?

The first users are retail investors who already have investment ideas but do not have the technical skill or time to convert those ideas into systematic strategies.

## What problem does it solve?

Trading apps made execution easy, but validation is still hard. Many users go directly from idea to buy/sell action without knowing whether the idea worked historically, when it failed, or how risky it was.

## What is the next product milestone?

The next milestone is a simple web MVP where a user can enter one investment idea and receive a strategy validation report with backtest, risk, failure points, and AI improvement suggestions.
