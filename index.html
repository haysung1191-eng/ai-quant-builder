The problem

Trading is too easy

Retail apps make buy and sell buttons instant, but most users still trade from intuition.

Validation is too hard

Backtesting, drawdown analysis, and strategy improvement are still technical workflows.

Risk is invisible

Investors often do not know when their idea fails, how large losses can get, or whether costs erase the edge.

The solution

Idea to strategy

Users write an investment idea in plain language. AI converts it into testable strategy logic.

Strategy to report

The product runs backtests, risk checks, failure-period analysis, and out-of-sample validation.

Report to better decision

Users get improvement suggestions before they make a trade. The first product is research-first, not advice-first.
